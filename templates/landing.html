FAQ

Answers to common questions.

Still wondering? Reach out — we usually reply within an hour.

Is HEALTHAPP free to use?
Yes — patients can register and book services for free. Providers (doctors, labs, pharmacies) get the dashboard at no cost during the launch phase.
How is my medical data protected?
All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Access is role-based and every action is logged. We follow HIPAA-aligned and GDPR-compatible practices.
How quickly can I book a doctor or lab visit?
Most home visits are confirmed in under 15 minutes and fulfilled the same day. You'll see live ETA tracking once a provider accepts your request.
Can I use HEALTHAPP as a doctor, lab, or pharmacy?
Absolutely. Register with your professional role — once verified by our admin team, you'll get a dedicated dashboard tailored to your workflow.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes — HEALTHAPP is fully responsive and works on every modern phone, tablet, and desktop browser. Native apps are coming soon.
